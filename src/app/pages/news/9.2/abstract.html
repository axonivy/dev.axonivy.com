OpenAPI power

We believe that the OpenAPI initiative made communications over system boundaries efficient, well documented and a pleasure to do. So, we gladly comply with it by documenting REST services by default in OpenAPI format. Nevertheless, we made the consumption of OpenAPI documented services joyful and easy so that you can instantly involve powerful OpenAPI services with zero technical burden.
