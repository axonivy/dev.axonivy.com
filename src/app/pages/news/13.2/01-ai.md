## Agentic Orchestration & AI Process Generation {#ai}

Version 13.2 expands the AI portfolio (IDP, Portal Assistant, LLM connectors) with two deeply integrated platform components: Smart Workflow and Smart Core.

- **Smart Workflow**: Smart Workflow embeds agents directly into Axon Ivy, combining deterministic BPMN steps with adaptive AI decision-making. It enables flexible orchestration where processes interact seamlessly with agent intelligence.
    
- **Smart Core**: Smart Core adds a native MCP server to the Axon Ivy Engine, enabling the generation of Ivy artifacts—processes, data classes, forms, connectors—directly from natural language.
