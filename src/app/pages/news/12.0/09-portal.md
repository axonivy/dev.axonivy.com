## Portal {#portal}

Welcome to the next level of productivity, efficiency, and accessibility with the release of Axon Ivy Portal LTS 12! This version marks a significant milestone, combining all the powerful features and enhancements from the 11.x Leading Edge releases into one robust, stable version. With LTS 12, we’re delivering the most intuitive, accessible, and customizable portal experience yet. Here’s everything that’s new:

### Enhanced Usability Features

LTS 12 introduces a redesigned user interface, merging functionality with a clean, modern look to make your work smoother and more efficient. Customization is at the heart of this release, allowing each user to create a portal that fits their needs:
- **Customizable Navigation**: Top-level navigation can now feature your personalized dashboards, making essential tools and information instantly accessible.
- **Enhanced Task and Case Widgets**: Adapt each widget to fit your workflow with customizable grids, flexible layouts, and options for custom fields, columns, and filters.
- **Responsive Across Devices**: Optimized for all screen sizes, the portal adapts seamlessly from desktops to mobile devices, making it easier than ever to stay productive on the go.
- New and modern look and feel.

### Meet Your New AI Assistant

Transform the way you interact with the portal using the AI Assistant. Acting as your virtual partner, the AI Assistant provides dynamic support across a range of tasks, from finding documents to managing tasks and processes. It’s like having a personal guide to streamline and enhance your workflow:
- **Intelligent Workflow Interaction**: Experience a more intuitive workflow by conversing with the assistant to navigate tasks, explore options, and make complex processes feel effortless.
- **Multilingual Support**: Communicate with ease in English, German, Spanish, and more, enabling a flexible experience in your preferred language.
- **Custom Models for Unique Needs**: Create personalized AI models to match your specific workflow requirements, assign behaviors, and build unique workflows for a tailor-made portal experience.

### Enhanced Widget Behavior

Stay informed with real-time notifications and dynamic, interactive widgets, keeping you in sync with your workflow:

- **Instant Notifications**: With customizable notification channels, users can manage preferences to stay informed of important tasks and deadlines.
- **Interactive Widgets**: From task to case and process widgets, LTS 12 makes it easier to manage your tasks in one streamlined environment. Use the advanced filtering options, apply custom fields, and enjoy lazy loading for seamless data access.
- **Quick Search in Widgets**: Locate any task or case instantly within the task and case widgets, using a customizable search scope for fast, precise results.
- **Global Search Overhaul**: The redesigned global search offers a clearer, faster experience, enabling you to pinpoint the exact information you need without delay.

### Comprehensive Accessibility Upgrades

Accessibility in the Axon Ivy Portal has been enhanced to support inclusivity and ensure usability for all users. By adhering to the WCAG 2.1 standards, LTS 12 offers a portal experience that’s optimized for users of all abilities:

- **Optimized Contrast**: Enjoy improved text, button, and background contrast across both Light and Dark modes for increased readability and visibility.
- **Enhanced Zoom and Layout Adjustments***: With optimized zoom capabilities on desktop and mobile, the portal layout maintains clarity and usability for users requiring larger text and elements.
- **Keyboard-Friendly Navigation**: Effortlessly navigate the portal with new keyboard shortcuts for quick access to sections like Dashboard, Process, Task, Case, and Statistics. Manage tasks, cases, and processes using intuitive shortcuts within each widget.
- **Screen Reader Support**: Screen reader enhancements ensure users with visual impairments can navigate with ease, with additional descriptive elements providing detailed task information.

### Enhanced Statistics

Take advantage of upgraded statistics capabilities and customizable charts to gain insights, all powered by Elasticsearch for top-notch performance. It is now possible to add a wide range of new statistics-based widgets, including:
- Tasks by Priority
- Top Priority: 3 Days
- Tasks by Priority
- Case Category Avg. Runtime
- New Cases Per Day
- Completed Cases Per Day
- Running Cases
- Tasks that expire by the end of the day
- Tasks by Priority
- Open Tasks
- Tasks Due Today

Additionally, the Statistics API of the Ivy Engine allows you to prepare your own statistics according to your use case. With the dashboard approach, you can build your own statistics dashboards tailored to your preferences and use cases.

### Open Source

With LTS 12, we’re excited to share that the Axon Ivy Portal is now fully open source! This opens up endless possibilities for developers to explore, innovate, and contribute to the portal’s evolution.

With Axon Ivy Portal LTS 12, your workspace is more powerful, adaptable, and accessible than ever. Dive into the latest features, make the portal your own, and elevate your productivity to new heights. Welcome to the future of the Axon Ivy Portal!
