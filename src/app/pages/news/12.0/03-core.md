## Core {#core}

- **Notification**: Task and business notifications are displayed in the Portal or are sent via mail or MS Teams. Send your own business notifications over a new API.
- **CRON**: You can now configure periodical jobs by using CRON expressions. CRON expressions give you a broad spectrum of possible configurations. From once every minute to every day, week, month, or year.
- **S3 Document Store**: Workflow documents can now be saved in S3-compatible storage. S3 has many advantages over the conventional local file system.
